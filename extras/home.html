ABOUT US

The main goal of our application is to ensure the accuracy of the availability and the storage location of the product
so that it can be easier to access and to check for availability for customers.
Thus, this system is directed towards owners of small to large stores and stock managers
who are responsible of maintaining sufficient goods in a retail or manufacturing business.
Our intention is to reduce the amount of time it takes to update a database manually.
